## Why Read the Quran Online?

The Noble Quran is the most widely read book in the world. With over 1.8 billion Muslims worldwide, access to the Quran in your own language is more important than ever.

Kuran.studio makes it possible to read the Quran in **over 80 languages**, completely free and without any ads or distractions.

## Getting Started

Reading the Quran on Kuran.studio is simple:

1. **Choose your language** — Select from 80+ translations in languages including English, French, Turkish, Albanian, Spanish, German, and many more.
2. **Pick a Surah** — Browse all 114 surahs with their Arabic names and translations.
3. **Start reading** — Each ayah (verse) shows the original Arabic text alongside the translation in your chosen language.

## Popular Surahs to Start With

If you're new to reading the Quran, these surahs are great starting points:

- **Al-Fatiha (Surah 1)** — The Opening, recited in every prayer
- **Ya-Sin (Surah 36)** — Often called the "heart of the Quran"
- **Ar-Rahman (Surah 55)** — The Most Merciful, known for its beautiful repetition
- **Al-Mulk (Surah 67)** — The Sovereignty, recommended for nightly reading
- **Al-Ikhlas (Surah 112)** — The Sincerity, a concise declaration of monotheism
